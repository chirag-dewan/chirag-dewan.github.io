MCP-Poison-Bench
A de-circularized study of MCP tool-poisoning across 4 injection classes, 6 models, and 3 vendors. The client-side tool_transform defense's apparent win was circular: on a held-out payload set authored to trip zero rules the reduction vanishes, and rug_pull (result-borne injection) bypasses the defense by design (1.00 → 1.00). Susceptibility tracks capability, not vendor.

KESTREL
